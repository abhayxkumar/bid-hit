About Us

This site will provide a real time responsive platform, where a person (bidder & seller) can create his account and authenticate himself with any of its id proof. This authentication will help to determine whether the person(bidder/seller) is real or not. And if the user is seller, first he have to upload its legal papers, so that the product/ or any property/ antique can be verified and considered as genuine and that information will be encrypted end to end so that frauds can be prevented. And also he can select a time slot in which the auction starts and end and also he can show its base bidding amount. If the person is bidder, he can find the things in which he is interest to bid, also he will also be given different time slots according to seller so that bidder can select the time slot so that he can bid when he is free. In this real time platform, the bidder will enjoy his bidding with an feasible responsive user interface. Also after the completion of auction, we will provide proper feasible payment methods so that bidder can transact the seller with a very encrypted way.
